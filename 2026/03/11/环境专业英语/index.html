语句翻译（记录一些实在翻译的很不好的句子）

1.Notably, the smelting wastewater is nitrogen-deficient due to the production process and operating conditions.

值得注意的是，冶炼废水因生产工艺及运行条件限制普遍存在氮元素缺乏问题。

2.Therefore, the conventional microbial Cr(VI) reduction does not work in smelting wastewater.

因此，传统微生物铬（VI）还原技术无法应用于冶炼废水处理。

3.Although the supplement of exogenous introgen sources such as ammonia and nitrate can address the issue, it increases the cost.

虽然外加氨、硝酸盐等氮源可解决此问题，但会增加处理成本。

4.Alarmingly, high O3 episodes - where daily maximum concentrations exceed ambient air quailty standards of 100 ppbv - are frequently observed in key regions such as the North China Plain, the Yangtze River Delta, and the Pearl River Delta(PRD).

令人担忧地是，华北平原、长江三角洲、珠江三角洲等重点区域频繁出现高浓度O3事件——日最高浓度值屡次突破100ppbv的环境空气标准。

​ episodes /ˈepɪsəʊd/ 事件

​ the North China Plain 华北平原

​ the Yangtze River Delta 长江三角洲

​ the Pearl River Delta(PRD) 珠江三角洲

​ daily maximum concentrations 日最高浓度值

​ ppbv 每亿份气体中有1份挥发性有机物质（VOCs）的浓度

5.This surge in O3 pollution is driven by multiple factors, including uncoordinated controls of VOCs and NOx emissions, reduced aerosol uptake of reactive gases, changing meteorological conditions, and increasing transboundary transport of pollutants.

臭氧污染由多重因素驱动，包括挥发性有机物和氮氧化物管控失衡、气溶胶对活性气体吸收能力下降、气象条件的变化以及污染物跨境传输加剧。

​ uncoordinated controls of VOCs and NOx emissions 挥发性有机物和氮氧化物管控失衡 —— uncoordinated 不协调的

​ reduced aerosol uptake of reactive gases 气溶胶对活性气体吸收能力下降 —— uptake 吸收，reactive gases 活性气体

​ changing meteorological conditions 气象条件的变化 —— meteorological /ˌmiːtiərəˈlɒdʒɪk(ə)l/ 气象学的

​ increasing transboundary transport of pollutants 污染物跨境传输加剧 —— transboundary /trænsˈbaʊndəri/ 跨界的
